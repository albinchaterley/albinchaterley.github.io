Perscrutarium Vitae · in fieri · per aspera

My name is N'gedoua. A secondary school student in Seconde, preparing for the Bac D in Côte d'Ivoire. I am driven by a single conviction: biology is not a discipline — it is a way of reading the world. This portfolio documents that reading. Unfinished. Always in progress.

cf. Renovatio Intra · 2026

Perscrutarium Vitae — literally, "the examination of life" — is a personal biology research project. Each entry follows a rigorous methodology: video exploration → M&R analysis → critical discussion → final report in English. This portfolio is its public trace.

Day 1 → Video + summary + light discussion
Day 2 → Methods & Results
Day 3 → Discussion + structured report (EN)

Observation II — Project Briefing

About

Fig. 2.0 — Project Documentation

What is this?

A structured personal biology research portfolio. Each project follows the PV-001 protocol and contributes to a university application dossier. A scientific log and a record of intellectual growth.

PV-001
Why does it exist?

Because curiosity deserves a method. Because the road to medicine or molecular biology begins here — in a notebook, on a phone screen, in Côte d'Ivoire.

Obs. II

Claude acts as a multidisciplinary consultant — challenging hypotheses, validating reasoning, structuring discussions. Claude never generates creative ideas for the research itself. The intellectual ownership is entirely N'gedoua's.

PV-001 — Methodology —

Day 1
Exploration. Watch a reference video or read a paper. Write a summary. Light discussion with Claude to clarify concepts.
Day 2
Analysis. Deep dive into Methods & Results. Understand the experimental design, the data, the conclusions.
Day 3
Synthesis. Critical discussion. Write a structured report in English — the core deliverable for the university dossier.
